# Newcastle Permanent Building Society

> Newcastle Permanent Building Society is a customer-owned Australian authorised deposit-taking institution (ADI), founded in 1903 and headquartered in Newcastle, NSW, and part of Newcastle Greater Mutual Group (NGM Group) since its 2023 merger with Greater Bank. As a Consumer Data Right (CDR) data holder it exposes a live, public, unauthenticated Product Reference Data (PRD) API conforming to the DSB Consumer Data Standards, alongside the "NPBS Innovation Sandbox and Marketplace" developer portal.

## APIs

- [CDR Product Reference Data API](https://developer.newcastlepermanent.com.au/): Public, unauthenticated CDR Product Reference Data — banking product catalogue (term deposits, savings, transaction accounts, home loans, credit cards) under /cds-au/v1/banking/products. Confirmed live, x-v 4, 41 products.
